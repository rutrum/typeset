Unlike sidenotes, these don't have reference numbers. Usage: {% marginnote() %} Your margin note content here. **Markdown** is supported. {% end %} The `nth` variable is automatically provided by Zola and increments for each shortcode invocation, ensuring unique IDs. #} {{ body | markdown(inline=true) | safe }}
